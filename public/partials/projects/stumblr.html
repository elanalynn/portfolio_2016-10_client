Stumblr is pub crawl planning app that maps a barhopping route based on your current location or a specific address.

Features include:

  • Login with Twitter Auth
  • Customize tweet settings and dashboard settings
  • Create a route based on location and route preferences
  • Save routes and see their statuses
  • View your route on a Google Map
  • Check in, skip and focus on bars on the map
  • Earn badges based on the type and number of routes you complete or forfeit
  • Tweet when you check in and earn badges
  • Connect to Uber when you end a route.

This was a collaboration with two other students. It was our first exposure to React, MongoDB and OpenShift.
