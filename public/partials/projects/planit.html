Planit is an event human resources marketplace. As a user, you act as an event owner or contributor. As an owner, you can create events, add tasks, review (and accept or reject) proposals. As a contributor, you can propose to other users' tasks based the qualifications listed in your profile.

This was a group project completed during the tenth week of bootcamp. My team took the opportunity to develop a granular Agile workflow and experience pair programming. We also built an SPA without a framework. I wouldn't recommend this, but it was a great learning experience. While I collaborated on many of the pieces, the majority of my focus was on the views and client-side javascript.
